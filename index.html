Our Services

Setting Up Fishing ponds to Farmers

Samaki Bora Company assesses the farmer's land to determine the best location for the fish pond and constructs it to industry standards with appropriate size and infrastructure, ensuring sufficient water supply and drainage systems for the farmed species.

Availing Fish Fingerlings

Samaki Bora Company provides high-quality, disease-free fish fingerlings and guidance on their handling and transport for farmers interested in fish farming. The service supports sustainable and profitable fish farming in the region.

Supplying Fish Feeds

Samaki Bora Company supplies fish feed to farmers engaged in fish farming. The company offers a variety of high-quality fish feeds that are suitable for different fish species and stages of growth.

Buying Fish from our Farmers

This service provides farmers with a guaranteed market for their fish and helps them earn a fair price for their products.

Conducting Fish Farming Trainings & Mentorship

The company offers guidance on best practices for fish farming, including pond construction, fish stocking, feeding, and disease management.
